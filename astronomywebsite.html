What is Astronomy?

In a brief definition astronomy is the study of celestial objects. A celestial object is what you would consider a planet, stars, galaxies, and other bodies such as black holes and quasars which you can find more basic information about at vedantu.com

Importance of Astronomy

Since 3,000 years ago and before that, mankind have wondered where we came from, how our own planet Earth works, how the laws of physics and other universal forces work under different parts of our solar system under different conditions.

The impact of astronomers is truly astonishing, they expand our knowledge about the vast universe we reside in. We gain insight and understanding about the structure, composotion and how a planet behaves during different parts of their respective "year".

Studying astronomy gives us knowledge about evolution and other things like our origin and the cosmic origin of different planets, stars, galaxies and other celestial bodies.
